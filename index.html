About

Every since I was a young boy I was fascinated by computers and any kind of electronic gadget. After a career in the consumer electronics business, I got a chance to return to that curiosity of computers by attending the Eleven Fifty Academy immersive cyber security bootcamp. It began with learning networking and getting my CompTIA Network+ certification. Next was a deep dive into the technical side of cyber security and earning my CompTIA Security+ certification. Then we used our new skills on the cyber range to prevent, monitor, and stop real cyber attacks.

Now I am attending the Eleven Fifty Academy Software Development bootcamp where I am learning to develop Full-Stack applications using Microsoft .NET technologies.
